- India-first mid-market and enterprise organizations with internet-facing applications, APIs, and customer portals.
- High-value targets in fintech, SaaS, logistics, healthcare, education, and regulated services.
- Security and platform leaders facing at least one of these constraints:

  - Rising incident response times and audit pressure.
- Typical buying committee: CISO/Head of Security, CTO/VP Engineering, Infra/SRE Lead, Procurement.

## 90-Day Pilot Scope

### Days 1-14, Baseline and Instrumentation
- Discovery across applications, attack surface, existing controls, and incident history.
- Security telemetry normalization and dashboard baseline.
- Risk register with prioritized remediation backlog.

### Days 15-45, Control Deployment and Tuning
- WAF/API/DDoS policy implementation and tuning.
- False-positive reduction and rule hardening.
- Incident response workflow integration with engineering and SOC.

### Days 46-75, Validation and Drill Cycles
- Controlled attack simulation and playbook validation.
- SLA measurement for detection, triage, and containment.
- Audit-ready evidence capture and control gap closure.

### Days 76-90, Executive Readout and Annualization Plan
- KPI outcomes and quantified risk-reduction report.
- 12-month roadmap with staffing and control milestones.
- Commercial proposal for annual managed program.

## Pilot Success Metrics
- Mean time to detect (MTTD) reduced by 30 percent or more.
- Mean time to contain (MTTC) reduced by 25 percent or more.
- Critical false positives reduced by 40 percent or more.
- 100 percent coverage for agreed internet-facing production endpoints.
- At least one completed cross-functional incident drill with postmortem.
- Executive scorecard accepted by security and engineering leadership.

## Security Checklist (Pre-Go-Live + Pilot Controls)
- [ ] Asset inventory finalized for all in-scope apps, APIs, and edge endpoints.
- [ ] DNS, TLS, and certificate ownership verified.
- [ ] Logging pipeline enabled for edge, application, and identity events.
- [ ] WAF baseline rules applied and exception process documented.
- [ ] DDoS response thresholds and contacts tested.
- [ ] API authentication, rate limiting, and abuse controls enforced.
- [ ] SIEM/SOAR integration validated with sample detections.
- [ ] On-call escalation matrix approved across Security and Engineering.
- [ ] Backup and rollback procedure tested for policy changes.
- [ ] Compliance evidence folder structure established.

## Pilot-to-Annual Conversion Triggers
- Pilot KPI targets met or exceeded for two consecutive reporting cycles.
- Leadership sign-off that incident response confidence materially improved.
- Demonstrated annualized cost avoidance greater than 2x annual contract value.
- Security backlog burn-down trend proves control ownership is sustainable.
- Audit/compliance reviewers accept pilot evidence with no critical gaps.
- Agreement on 12-month roadmap, governance cadence, and QBR schedule.
